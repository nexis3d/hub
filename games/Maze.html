The Spooky Maze

HOW TO PLAY

  • Goal: Navigate the labyrinth and find the glowing green exit.
  • Controls: Use W, A, S, D to move. Use your Mouse to look around.
  • Defense: Left-Click to shoot your Blaster. It will banish the monster temporarily.
  • Warning: You have limited ammo! Use it wisely. The monster will always return.
