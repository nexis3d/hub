💻 PC Keyboard

  • P1: A/D Move, W Jump, S Attack
  • P2: ←/→ Move, ↑ Jump, ↓ Attack
  • P3: J/L Move, I Jump, K Attack

📱 Touch Screen

  • Jump: Drag Joystick Up
  • Attack: Drag Joystick Down
  • Pause: Hold top-center button
