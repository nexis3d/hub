THE ARCHITECT'S CURSE

GOTHIC ESCAPE ROOM HORROR
In 1892, a brilliant architect sealed his family inside this 3-story gothic manor, filling it with traps and an intelligent clockwork Automaton to "protect" them. You have 10 minutes before the structural locks engage permanently.

Read the clues to piece together the 5-digit safe code. You must explore the open rooms to find the hidden keys inside cabinets. The keys will unlock the Parkour Wing, where the Safe containing the Master Key resides.
Hide under beds and in closets. The Automaton is hunting you. Use the Mini-map to track your location.
