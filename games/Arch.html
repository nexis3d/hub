SURVIVAL GUIDE

Objective: Deduce the 5-digit safe code by reading Architect's Journals scattered across the estate. Find Keys in cabinets to unlock the Attic. Escape the front door. Break open locked doors with a Crowbar.

  • [ SPACE ] - Jump

  • [ LEFT CLICK ] - Use Item (Swing Crowbar/Bat)

The East Lounge is the Safe Zone. Find coins to buy defenses there. If you hide in closets or under beds for too long outside the safe zone, the Director will alert the Automaton.
